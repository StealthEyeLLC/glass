# Launch distribution (bounded showcase)

Keep the launch narrow, replay-first, and proof-heavy. Reuse the same language everywhere. Do not broaden the claim to hosted live mode, cloud Glass, production ingest, or final F-IPC.

## Core framing

Glass is a replay-first bounded investigation surface: one honest path from scene to change to evidence to receipt, with optional local live mode kept local-only and secondary.

## Anti-hype guardrails

- Do not call Glass an observability platform.
- Do not imply cloud hosting or hosted live mode.
- Do not imply full topology/runtime understanding.
- Do not add “AI explains your runtime” language.
- Prefer one honest example over a feature list.
